<p><p>Mobile telescopic cranes are categorized mainly into two types: wheeled telescopic cranes and crawler telescopic cranes. Wheeled telescopic cranes are mounted on wheels, offering high mobility and quick setup for urban and construction sites with firm surfaces. In contrast, crawler telescopic cranes are equipped with tracks, providing enhanced stability and load-bearing capacity on uneven terrain. Both types serve diverse applications in the construction and logistics sectors, balancing maneuverability and operational efficiency based on site conditions.</p></p>
