<p><p>The Interstitial Cystitis Drugs Market is primarily divided into two types: Oral Therapy and Intravesical Therapy. Oral Therapy includes medications taken by mouth to manage pain and urinary urgency, such as antihistamines and pentosan polysulfate sodium. Intravesical Therapy involves delivering treatments directly into the bladder, providing localized relief. This method typically uses agents like dimethyl sulfoxide or hyaluronic acid, targeting inflammation and promoting bladder healing. Both therapies aim to alleviate symptoms and improve the quality of life for patients.</p></p>
