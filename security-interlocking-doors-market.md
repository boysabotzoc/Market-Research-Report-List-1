<p><p>The security interlocking doors market comprises two main types: automatic and non-return doors. Automatic interlocking doors enhance security by allowing seamless entry while preventing unauthorized access. They use advanced sensors and mechanisms to ensure simultaneous opening and closing, improving traffic flow in secured areas. Non-return interlocking doors, on the other hand, are designed to maintain a controlled environment by preventing reverse entry. This mechanism ensures that once an individual exits, they cannot re-enter without proper authorization, enhancing overall security measures.</p></p>
