<p><p>The Drug Metabolism and Pharmacokinetics (DMPK) service market comprises two primary types: In Vivo and In Vitro services. In Vivo DMPK services involve studying the absorption, distribution, metabolism, and excretion (ADME) of drugs within living organisms, providing insights into how a drug behaves in a biological system. In contrast, In Vitro DMPK services focus on analyzing metabolic pathways and kinetics using isolated cells or tissues, offering a controlled environment to predict drug behavior and interactions without the complexity of whole organisms.</p></p>
