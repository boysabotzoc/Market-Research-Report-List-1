<p><p>Edge computing refers to a decentralized computing architecture that brings computation and data storage closer to the location where it is needed, reducing latency and improving response times. By processing data near the source, edge computing enhances the performance of applications and enables real-time analytics, making it crucial for IoT devices, autonomous vehicles, and smart city infrastructures.</p><p>The Edge Computing System Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.2% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by the increasing demand for faster data processing and the proliferation of IoT devices. Industries such as telecommunications, healthcare, manufacturing, and transportation are adopting edge computing solutions to enhance operational efficiency and reduce dependence on centralized cloud services.</p><p>Key trends shaping the market include the integration of artificial intelligence and machine learning at the edge, enabling smarter decision-making processes, and the emergence of 5G technology, which enhances connectivity and supports more extensive edge computing infrastructures. Additionally, security concerns surrounding data privacy are prompting companies to adopt edge computing to ensure sensitive information is processed closer to its source, thus mitigating risks associated with data transfer to centralized servers.</p></p>
