<p><strong>The Kraft Sealing Tape Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water Activated Kraft Paper Tape</li><li>Water Free Kraft Paper Tape</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Kraft sealing tape market is primarily divided into two types: water-activated Kraft paper tape and water-free Kraft paper tape. Water-activated Kraft paper tape requires moisture to bond effectively, creating a strong seal ideal for heavy-weight packaging and eco-friendly applications. In contrast, water-free Kraft paper tape utilizes a pressure-sensitive adhesive, allowing for easier application and quick sealing without the need for water. Both types are popular for their durability and sustainability in shipping and packaging solutions.</p></p>
