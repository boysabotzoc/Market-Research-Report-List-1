<p><p>The synthetic surgical sealant market comprises various types, including cyanoacrylates, polyethylene glycol hydrogels, and urethane-based adhesives. Cyanoacrylates offer rapid bonding and are ideal for skin and tissue closure. Polyethylene glycol hydrogels provide a biocompatible option, useful for soft tissue applications due to their flexibility and moisture retention. Urethane-based adhesives combine strength with elasticity, suitable for diverse surgical procedures. Other types include novel bioadhesives and fibrin-based products, enhancing versatility in surgical applications and improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
