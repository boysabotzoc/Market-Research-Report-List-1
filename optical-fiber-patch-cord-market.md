<p><ul><li>Single-mode Fiber Optical Patch Cord</li><li>Multimode Fiber Optical Patch Cord</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical fiber patch cord market comprises two primary types: single-mode and multimode fiber optical patch cords. Single-mode fiber patch cords utilize a single light path, enabling long-distance data transmission with minimal signal loss, ideal for telecommunication and high-bandwidth applications. In contrast, multimode fiber patch cords support multiple light paths, allowing for shorter distance data transfers, commonly used in local area networks and data centers. Both types are crucial for efficient network connectivity and signal integrity in various environments.</p></p>
