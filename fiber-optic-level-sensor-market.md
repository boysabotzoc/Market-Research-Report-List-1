<p><p>The Fiber Optic Level Sensor market is categorized into contact and contactless types. Contact sensors directly interact with the material being measured, providing precise level readings for liquids and solids via fiber optics, which enhance accuracy and reliability. In contrast, contactless sensors operate without physical contact, using light to detect levels at a distance, minimizing contamination risks and wear. Each type caters to different application needs, with varying benefits related to environmental conditions and measurement precision.</p></p>
