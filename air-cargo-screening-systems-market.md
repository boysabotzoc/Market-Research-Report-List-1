<p><ul><li>Explosive Detection Systems (EDS)</li><li>Explosive Trace Detection (ETD)</li><li>Non-computed Tomography Transmission X-ray (Non-CT X-ray)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air cargo screening systems market comprises several key types: Explosive Detection Systems (EDS) are advanced technologies designed to identify hidden explosives within cargo, ensuring aviation security. Explosive Trace Detection (ETD) systems detect minute traces of explosive materials on surfaces or in the air, providing a sensitive means of threat detection. Non-computed Tomography Transmission X-ray (Non-CT X-ray) systems utilize standard X-ray imaging to visualize the contents of cargo, enabling quick assessments while maintaining security protocols. Together, these technologies enhance safety in air transport.</p></p>
