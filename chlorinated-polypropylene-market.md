<p><p>Chlorinated Polypropylene (CPP) is categorized into two primary market types: Type I and Type II. Type I CPP is characterized by its high chlorine content, offering enhanced chemical resistance and adhesion properties, making it suitable for coatings, adhesives, and sealants. Type II CPP has a lower chlorine concentration, providing flexibility and improved processing characteristics, often used in applications like films and laminates. Both types serve diverse industries, including automotive, construction, and packaging, fulfilling specific functional requirements.</p></p>
