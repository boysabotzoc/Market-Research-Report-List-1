<p><p>The MBS resin market is categorized into **Transparent MBS** and **Non-Transparent MBS** types. Transparent MBS resin is used in applications requiring clarity, such as packaging, films, and transparent sheets, offering high impact strength and optical properties. Non-Transparent MBS resin is utilized in opaque products like pipes, fittings, and industrial components, providing excellent toughness and durability. Both types enhance material performance, with transparency being the key differentiator based on application requirements.</p></p>
