<p><strong>The Inkjet Printer Head Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Piezoelectric Type</li><li>Thermal Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The inkjet printer head market is primarily divided into two types: piezoelectric and thermal. Piezoelectric printer heads use electric charges to control the deformation of piezoelectric materials, enabling precise droplet formation, which is ideal for high-quality images and various ink types. In contrast, thermal printer heads rely on heat to create vapor bubbles in ink, forcing droplets onto the paper, which is commonly found in consumer printers due to its cost-effectiveness. Both types cater to different printing needs and applications.</p></p>
