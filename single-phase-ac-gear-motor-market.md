<p><strong>The Single-Phase AC Gear-Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Synchronous</li><li>Asynchronous</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The single-phase AC gear-motor market is primarily divided into synchronous and asynchronous types. Synchronous gear-motors operate at a constant speed in sync with the AC supply frequency, ensuring precise control and applications requiring steady torque. Asynchronous gear-motors, on the other hand, operate at varying speeds and slip, making them more versatile and widely used in various industries due to their ability to tolerate load variations. Both types offer distinct advantages based on application needs and efficiency requirements.</p></p>
