<p><p>The Safety Hypodermic Needles market is divided into two main types: Active Safety Needles and Passive Safety Needles. Active Safety Needles require user intervention to activate the safety feature, typically involving a mechanism that is triggered after the injection. In contrast, Passive Safety Needles automatically engage their safety features without requiring additional action from the user, reducing the risk of needle stick injuries. Both types aim to enhance safety for healthcare professionals and patients while minimizing potential exposure to contaminated needles.</p></p>
