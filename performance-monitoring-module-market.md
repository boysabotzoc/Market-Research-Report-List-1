<p><strong>The Performance Monitoring Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>OPM</li><li>OTDR</li><li>OLTS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Performance Monitoring Module market encompasses three main types: OPM (Optical Power Meter), OTDR (Optical Time-Domain Reflectometer), and OLTS (Optical Loss Test Set). OPMs measure optical power levels in fiber networks, vital for assessing performance. OTDRs analyze signal reflections to determine fiber integrity and locate faults. OLTSs evaluate optical loss by measuring the transmitted and received power levels, ensuring compliance with network standards. Together, these tools are essential for maintaining and optimizing fiber optic networks in various applications.</p></p>
