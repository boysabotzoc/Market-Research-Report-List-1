<p><ul><li>Smoothing Coating</li><li>Filling Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Surface Preparation Coating market is divided into two main types: Smoothing Coating and Filling Coating. Smoothing Coating aims to create a uniform surface finish, enhancing aesthetic appeal and ensuring better adhesion for subsequent coatings. It levels out imperfections and provides a sleek base. In contrast, Filling Coating is designed to fill in gaps, voids, and surface irregularities, offering a thicker layer that improves surface integrity. Both types are essential for optimizing surface quality in various applications.</p></p>
