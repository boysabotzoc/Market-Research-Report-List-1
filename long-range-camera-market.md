<p><p>The long-range camera market is primarily divided into two main types: long-range thermal cameras and non-thermal long-range cameras. Long-range thermal cameras detect infrared radiation, allowing for visibility in total darkness or adverse weather, making them ideal for security and surveillance applications. In contrast, non-thermal long-range cameras rely on visible light and are commonly used for tasks such as monitoring and reconnaissance in well-lit conditions. Each type serves specific needs across various industries, enhancing situational awareness and operational effectiveness.</p></p>
