<p><p>The self-climbing concrete placing boom market is categorized into two main types: climbing inside floors and elevator climb systems. Climbing inside floors utilize a mechanism that allows the boom to move vertically within the building's floors, enhancing efficiency in restricted spaces. On the other hand, elevator climb systems are designed to operate alongside elevators, facilitating material placement in high-rise structures while ensuring seamless integration with building logistics. Both types optimize concrete placement, reducing labor costs and increasing safety on construction sites.</p></p>
