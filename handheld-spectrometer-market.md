<p><p>The handheld spectrometer market encompasses two primary types: optical and electromagnetic spectrometers. Optical spectrometers utilize light to analyze the spectral composition of materials, making them ideal for applications in agriculture, art conservation, and environmental monitoring. In contrast, electromagnetic spectrometers detect a broader range of electromagnetic waves, including infrared and microwave, which are useful for chemical analysis and industrial applications. Both types offer portability, enhancing fieldwork efficiency and enabling on-site analysis across various industries.</p></p>
