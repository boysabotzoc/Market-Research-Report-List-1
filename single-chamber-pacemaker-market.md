<p><p>The single-chamber pacemaker market consists of two main types: temporary and permanent pacemakers. Temporary pacemakers are used for short-term cardiac support during medical procedures or emergencies, providing immediate pacing for patients with transient arrhythmias. In contrast, permanent pacemakers are implanted for long-term management of chronic heart conditions, ensuring consistent heart rhythm regulation. The choice between these types depends on the patient's condition, duration of pacing required, and the underlying cardiac issues being addressed.</p></p>
