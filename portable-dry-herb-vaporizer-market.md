<p><ul><li>Conduction Type</li><li>Convection Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable dry herb vaporizer market is primarily segmented into two types: conduction and convection vaporizers. Conduction vaporizers heat herbs directly through contact with a heated surface, offering quick heating times and compact designs. In contrast, convection vaporizers use hot air to evenly circulate heat around the herbs, resulting in more flavorful and efficient vaporization but typically requiring longer heating times and larger units. Both types cater to varying consumer preferences for efficiency, taste, and portability.</p></p>
