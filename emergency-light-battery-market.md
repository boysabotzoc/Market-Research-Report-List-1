<p><ul><li>NiCd battery</li><li>NiMH battery</li><li>Li-ion battery</li><li>Lead-acid battery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The emergency light battery market features several types, each with distinct characteristics. Nickel-Cadmium (NiCd) batteries are known for their durability and ability to perform in extreme temperatures, though they suffer from memory effect. Nickel-Metal Hydride (NiMH) batteries offer higher capacity and eco-friendliness, with less memory effect. Lithium-ion (Li-ion) batteries provide high energy density and longer life, making them increasingly popular. Lead-acid batteries, while heavier and less efficient, are cost-effective and reliable, often used in larger emergency lighting systems.</p></p>
