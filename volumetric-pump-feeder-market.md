<p><strong>The Volumetric Pump Feeder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Screw</li><li>Twin Screw</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The volumetric pump feeder market consists of two main types: single screw and twin screw feeders. Single screw feeders use a single helical screw to convey and dose materials, offering simplicity and efficiency for various applications. In contrast, twin screw feeders feature two intermeshing screws that provide enhanced control over material flow and are ideal for handling challenging materials with better accuracy. Both types cater to diverse industries, ensuring precise volumetric feeding in processes like plastics, food, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
