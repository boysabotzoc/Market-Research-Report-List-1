<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The DNA Methylation Sequencing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Whole Genome Bisulfite Sequencing (WGBS)</li><li>Reduced Representation Bisulfite Sequencing (RRBS)</li><li>MeDIP-Sequncing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>DNA methylation sequencing encompasses various techniques for analyzing methylation patterns in genomic DNA. Whole Genome Bisulfite Sequencing (WGBS) provides a comprehensive view of methylation across the entire genome, offering high-resolution data. Reduced Representation Bisulfite Sequencing (RRBS) focuses on a smaller subset of the genome, emphasizing regions of interest while reducing costs. MeDIP-Sequencing utilizes methylated DNA immunoprecipitation to enrich for methylated sequences, allowing for targeted analysis of specific regions, enhancing efficiency and depth in studying methylation landscapes.</p></p>
