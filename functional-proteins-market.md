<p><p>The functional proteins market comprises various types, including hydrolysates, whey protein concentrates, and whey protein isolates. Hydrolysates are pre-digested proteins that enhance absorption, making them ideal for sports nutrition. Whey protein concentrates contain a moderate level of protein and fats, often used in supplements and food products for muscle recovery. Whey protein isolates offer a higher protein content with minimal fats and carbohydrates, catering to those seeking low-calorie options. Each type serves specific dietary and functional needs within the protein market.</p></p>
