<p><ul><li>Meltblown Nonwoven</li><li>Spunbond Nonwoven</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spunmelt nonwovens market encompasses primarily two types: meltblown and spunbond nonwovens. Meltblown nonwovens are produced using high-velocity hot air to produce fine fibers, resulting in lightweight and highly absorbent materials, often used in filtration and medical applications. Spunbond nonwovens, on the other hand, involve extruding molten polymer through spinnerets to form continuous filaments, creating strong and durable fabrics suitable for a range of applications, including hygiene products, geotextiles, and packaging. Together, they cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
