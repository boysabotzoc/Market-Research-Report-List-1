<p><p>Saturable Absorber Mirrors (SAM) are vital components in laser technology, enabling mode-locking by allowing high-intensity light to pass while absorbing lower-intensity light. The market is divided into Resonant Saturable Absorber Mirrors (RSAM) and Non-resonant Saturable Absorber Mirrors. RSAMs use specific wavelength tuning for enhanced performance and stability, ideal for precise applications. In contrast, Non-resonant SAMs operate effectively across broader spectral ranges, catering to general laser systems, reflecting diverse demands in industrial, scientific, and medical applications.</p></p>
