<p><strong>The Magneto Resistive RAM Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Toggle MRAM</li><li>STT-MRAM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Magneto Resistive RAM (MRAM) market is primarily categorized into two types: Toggle MRAM and Spin-Transfer Torque MRAM (STT-MRAM). Toggle MRAM utilizes a toggle switching mechanism for data storage, offering fast write speeds and enhanced durability, making it suitable for applications requiring low power consumption. Conversely, STT-MRAM leverages spin-polarized current to manipulate magnetic layers, providing higher density and scalability. Both technologies are gaining traction for non-volatile memory applications, particularly in sectors like automotive, consumer electronics, and data centers.</p></p>
