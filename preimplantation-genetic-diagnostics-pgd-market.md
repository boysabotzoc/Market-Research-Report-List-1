<p><p>Preimplantation Genetic Diagnosis (PGD) is a reproductive technology used to identify genetic defects in embryos before implantation during in vitro fertilization (IVF). The PGD market primarily consists of two types: Preimplantation Genetic Screening (PGS) and Preimplantation Genetic Diagnosis. PGS focuses on assessing embryos for chromosomal abnormalities, enhancing the chances of a successful pregnancy. Meanwhile, PGD targets specific genetic diseases or disorders, allowing prospective parents to select embryos free from inherited conditions, thus improving overall reproductive outcomes.</p></p>
