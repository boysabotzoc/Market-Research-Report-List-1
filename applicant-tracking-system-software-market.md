<p><p>Applicant Tracking System (ATS) software is available in two primary market types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise ATS is installed locally on a company's servers, offering complete control over data security and customization, but requires significant IT resources for maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based ATS is hosted on external servers, providing flexibility, scalability, and easier updates without heavy upfront costs. This model is increasingly popular due to its accessibility from anywhere and reduced need for in-house IT support.</p></p>
