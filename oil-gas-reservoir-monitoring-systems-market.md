<p><p>Oil & Gas Reservoir Monitoring Systems encompass various technologies used to optimize and assess reservoir performance. Electrical downhole monitoring systems utilize sensors to gather data on pressure and temperature, providing real-time information on reservoir conditions. Fiber optic downhole monitoring systems leverage light transmission for high-resolution measurements of temperature and strain along wellbores. Wireless downhole communication monitoring systems facilitate data transmission without physical connections, enhancing operational flexibility.
