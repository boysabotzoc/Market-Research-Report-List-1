<p><p>The cutting mat market comprises two primary types: hard-surface (self-sealing) mats and self-healing mats. Hard-surface mats are designed for durability and provide a firm cutting surface, ideal for precision tasks. Self-healing mats, on the other hand, are made from specialized materials that close up after cuts are made, reducing visible wear and prolonging their lifespan. Both types cater to various crafts, hobbies, and professional applications, ensuring clean cuts while protecting work surfaces from damage.</p></p>
