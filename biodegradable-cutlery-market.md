<p><p>The biodegradable cutlery market comprises three main types: PLA (polylactic acid), CPLA (crystalized PLA), and starch blends. PLA cutlery is made from renewable resources like corn, offering compostability and a lower environmental impact. CPLA is a more heat-resistant version of PLA, suitable for hot food applications. Starch blends combine plant starch with other biodegradable materials, enhancing flexibility and strength. These products cater to the growing demand for sustainable and eco-friendly alternatives in the food service industry.</p></p>
