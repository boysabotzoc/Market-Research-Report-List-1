<p><ul><li>Non-Vehicular Aircraft EGTS</li><li>Vehicular Aircraft EGTS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Aircraft Electric Taxiing Systems (AETS) market is categorized into two main types: Non-Vehicular Aircraft Electric Ground Taxiing Systems (EGTS) and Vehicular Aircraft EGTS. Non-Vehicular Aircraft EGTS are integrated within the aircraft itself, utilizing onboard electric motors for taxiing without engine thrust, enhancing fuel efficiency and reducing emissions. In contrast, Vehicular Aircraft EGTS involves dedicated ground vehicles that assist in taxiing, providing external power and support. Both systems aim to improve operational efficiency and minimize environmental impact during ground movements.</p></p>
