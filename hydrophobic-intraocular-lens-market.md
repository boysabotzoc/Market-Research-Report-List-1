<p><p>The hydrophobic intraocular lens market can be segmented into double focus and three focus lenses. Double focus lenses, also known as bifocal lenses, offer two distinct focal points for near and distant vision, catering primarily to patients with presbyopia. In contrast, three focus lenses provide an additional focal point, enhancing intermediate vision, which is advantageous for tasks like using a computer. Both types aim to improve visual acuity and quality of life for cataract patients and those seeking vision correction.</p></p>
