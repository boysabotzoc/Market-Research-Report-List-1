<p><p>The medical antibacterial glass market is segmented into four main types. Soluble antimicrobial glass releases therapeutic ions to combat bacteria. Porous antimicrobial glass features small openings that enhance antibacterial efficacy through increased surface area and antimicrobial interactions. Coated antibacterial glass is treated with protective coatings that inhibit microbial growth. Ion diffusion antibacterial glass releases ions from within its structure to maintain a long-lasting antibacterial effect. Each type plays a crucial role in enhancing hygiene in medical environments.</p></p>
